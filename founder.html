The Problem

The $1.7T fashion industry makes seven-figure decisions blind.

A designer launches 50 SKUs from a trend forecast. A buyer commits millions from historical category data. An influencer drives awareness. A consumer buys — and then 15–40% comes back as returns.

The root cause is architectural: the consumer's wardrobe is invisible to everyone making decisions upstream.

The Moat — why now

Better intelligence → better performance → deeper integration → more data → stronger models.

At 50 enterprise customers, Sartori has visibility into billions of apparel interaction signals that improve fit prediction, demand forecasting, and recommendation accuracy across the entire platform.
