The Problem

The $1.7T fashion industry makes seven-figure decisions blind.

A designer launches 50 SKUs from a trend forecast. A buyer commits millions from historical category data. An influencer drives awareness. A consumer buys — and then 15–40% comes back as returns.

The root cause is architectural: the consumer's wardrobe is invisible to everyone making decisions upstream.

Annual margin destruction for a $100M brand

$60–160B

Estimated industry-wide annual loss from returns, markdowns, and misallocated inventory

The Solution

Nine portals. One wardrobe graph. Three intelligence layers.

Layer 1 · CX

Consumer Graph

Users log in; we map measurements, Style DNA, and ingest their actual wardrobe — what they own, wear, and return. The source of truth that powers everything else.

Layer 2 · DX BX IX

Creative & Commerce

Designers see missing categories. Brands see gap analysis across customers. Influencers see what's actually worn. The graph becomes commercial decisions.
